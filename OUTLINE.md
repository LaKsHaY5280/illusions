## SEGMENT 1: OPENING HOOK + WARM-UP (4 minutes)

**Purpose:** Instantly grab attention and get everyone participating via quick polls

**Format:** Rapid-fire polling mode (3 quick polls)

**Flow:**

1. Present tagline: _"Your brain is about to lie to you for 25 minutes."_
2. Display illusion image
3. Launch poll with question (20 seconds voting time)
4. Close poll & reveal live results
5. Show correct answer with brief explanation (15 seconds)
6. Move to next illusion

**Polls:**

### Poll 1: Penrose Triangle

- **Question:** "Is this object possible to build in real life?"
- **Options:** Yes / No / Not sure
- **Answer:** No
- **Explanation:** "This is an impossible object - it looks real in 2D but can't exist in 3D space!"
- **Time:** 20s vote + 15s reveal

### Poll 2: Rotating Snakes

- **Question:** "Does this image actually move?"
- **Options:** Yes / No / Can't tell
- **Answer:** No
- **Explanation:** "It's completely still! Your brain creates the illusion of movement from the pattern contrasts."
- **Time:** 20s vote + 15s reveal

### Poll 3: The Dress

- **Question:** "What colors do you see in the dress?"
- **Options:** Blue & Black / White & Gold / Other
- **Answer:** Both are correct (perceptual ambiguity)
- **Explanation:** "Both are valid! Your brain interprets the lighting differently."
- **Time:** 20s vote + 15s reveal

**Why this works:** Low barrier to entry, instant participation, creates energy and excitement, establishes polling rhythm

- **Time:** 20s vote + 15s reveal
